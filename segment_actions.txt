RFM Customer Segmentation Business Actions

Champions:
- Provide exclusive loyalty rewards
- Offer early access to new products
- Encourage referrals

Loyal Customers:
- Upsell premium products
- Cross-sell related items
- Offer membership programs

At Risk:
- Send re-engagement emails
- Provide limited-time discounts
- Conduct feedback surveys

Regular Customers:
- Run engagement campaigns
- Promote best-selling products
- Encourage repeat purchases
